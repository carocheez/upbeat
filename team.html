Jerry Chen 陳中恝 Founder

Prior to founding Upbeat, Jerry worked at Intel, Lucent Technology, Apple and Procket on CPU related projects. Later he worked at Inphi (NYSE: IPHI) as the Director of Marketing, focusing on founding High Speed Serdes product line. After Inphi, Jerry led the camera business unit at Genesys Logic(TWO:6104), successfully developed ISP technology from scratch designed-in ISP camera chip to Google, Lenovo, ASUS, and several Tier-1 companies in the world. Jerry worked as the VP of Marketing at Genesys Logic America as a major contributor on USB 3.0, SATA and MIPI specification. Jerry went to University of Texas at Austin ECE department and got his Master’s degree.
